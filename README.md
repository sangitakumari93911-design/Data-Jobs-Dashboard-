# # 📊 Data Jobs Dashboard | End-to-End Power BI Project

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project transforms raw data job market data into an interactive and business-focused Power BI dashboard. The solution provides actionable insights into hiring trends, salary benchmarks, job demand, and employee satisfaction metrics within the data industry.

Designed for data professionals, recruiters, and hiring managers, the dashboard enables users to explore market trends, compare compensation levels, and identify the most in-demand roles through interactive visualizations.

## 📌 Key Business Insights

* **Total Job Volume:** Analyze overall market demand through global job posting counts.
* **Salary Benchmarking:** Compare median annual salaries with hourly compensation rates across data roles.
* **Role Demand Analysis:** Identify the most sought-after positions such as Data Analyst, Data Engineer, and Data Scientist.
* **Job Satisfaction Metrics:** Monitor average job ratings and overall market sentiment.
* **Hiring Trends:** Track changes in job postings over time to uncover seasonal and long-term hiring patterns.

## 🛠 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* Total Job Count
* Average Job Rating
* Median Yearly Salary
* Median Hourly Salary

### Jobs Over Time

Interactive line chart showing hiring trends and fluctuations across different time periods.

### Salary Distribution Analysis

Scatter plot comparing hourly compensation against median annual salaries to identify high-paying job categories.

### Job Demand by Title

Horizontal bar chart ranking job roles by posting volume to highlight market demand.

### Job Statistics Matrix

Detailed tabular view with sparklines, enabling quick comparison of salaries, job counts, and trend patterns across roles.

## 💻 Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and data visualization
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and ETL processes
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Dynamic measures, KPIs, and calculations
* **Data Modeling** – Relationship management and analytical structure design

## 📈 Business Impact

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Understand current data job market trends
* Identify high-demand roles and career opportunities
* Benchmark compensation across job categories
* Support data-driven recruitment and career planning decisions
